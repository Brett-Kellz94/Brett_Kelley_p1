How it works:


  1. Fill out a new Tuition Request Form at least one week in advance of the course.
  2. Check the status of your form periodically.
  3. Once your request has been accepted by all the applicable parties, your award is pending until
    you submit your final grade and any other necessary documents.
  4. After your final grade is accepted, check your current rewards in the 'Rewards' tab.


Note:

You can attach an approval email from your supervisor, which can speed up the initial
approval process. You must submit a grade in order for tuition to be reimbursed.
If this grade is in the form of a presentation, you must attach the presentation for your direct supervisor to view.

You can claim up to $1000.00 annually in tuition reimburesement. If you have a valid claim
for more than the limit, you can attach a document outlining your claim for the Benefits Coordinator to review.
